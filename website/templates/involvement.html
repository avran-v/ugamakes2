SeaTrees

Help with coral reef restoration!

SeaTrees was founded in 2018 as a program of the non-profit organization Sustainable Surf to plant, protect, and restore blue carbon coastal ecosystems. Their custom blended carbon offset product, the SeaTrees Token, combines carbon credits with innovative blue carbon projects. Each SeaTrees Token purchased sequesters 1 ton of CO2 via Verified Carbon Standard certified offset projects, plants 4 mangrove trees, and creates 1 square foot of kelp forest.

The Ocean Foundation

Support the planting of seagrass!

Founded by Wolcott Henry in 2003 as a community foundation committed to reversing ocean degradation globally, the Ocean Foundation supports the planting of seagrass. Seagrass meadows are an important carbon sink and store 11% of the ocean’s buried carbon despite only accounting for only 0.1% of the world’s seafloor. Today, Ocean Foundation protects our oceans and coastal ecosystems through conservation initiatives, community foundation services, research and development, and blue carbon offsets.

Kelp Forest Foundation

Get involved in planting more kelp forests!

The “Kelp Forest Foundation” (KFF) is a Netherlands-registered non-profit charity with a vision of harnessing the power of giant kelp to help restore the health of the planet. Kelp forests are magical underwater habitats, known to be one of the most productive natural ecosystems on the planet. They are a key asset to naturally combat the effects of climate change and have a positive impact on a wide range of environmental challenges.
